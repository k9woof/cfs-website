Cookies

Last updated: 18/07/2026

As stated in our Privacy Policy we do not set tracking or advertsing cookies of any kind.

In fact this site uses only one cookie: cf_clearance. This cookie, as stated on Cloudflare's website is required for JavaScript Detections (a type of anti-bot challenge), and is strictly necessary and so does not require seperate consent under UK data protection law.

If the number or type of cookies used on this site changes we will update this page accordingly.
