Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14/07/2026

This website is operated by Craigellachie Filling Station Limited ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what information we collect from visitors and how it's used.

1. Hosting and Server Logs

This site is hosted using Cloudflare Pages. As part of standard hosting and security operations, Cloudflare may automatically process technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited. This data is collected for security, performance, and service-delivery purposes (e.g preventing abuse and ensuring the site loads correctly), and is processed by Cloudflare on our behalf as our data processor. We do not use this information to identify individual visitors, and we do not combine it with any other personal data. For more information on Cloudflare's own data practices, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

2. Cookies

We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking. Cloudflare may set limited, strictly necessary cookies (for example, for security and bot protection) as part of delivering the site. These are functional in nature and do not require seperate consent under UK data protection law.

3. Contact by Email or Telephone

If you contact us directly by email or telephone, we will use the personal information you provide (such as your name, email address, or telephone number) solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not add your details to any mailing list, and we do not share them with third parties, unless required by law. We will retain this information only for as long as necessary to deal with your enquiry, after which it will be deleted or anonymised.

4. Your Rights

Under UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete it
  • Object to how it's processed
To exercise any of these rights, contact us here.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
